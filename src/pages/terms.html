Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 13, 2021

Introduction

Wonolo Inc. and its affiliates (collectively “Wonolo”, “we”, or “us”) is a marketplace platform company
that provides an on-demand service (“Service”) that allows businesses (“Customers”) to connect with 
Wonolo’s network of geographically distributed independent contractors (“Wonoloers”) for various 
short-term assignments.

When you use our Service as a Customer or Wonoloer (collectively “user” or “you”), you share your 
information with us. We respect your privacy and want to be upfront about the information we collect,
how we use it, whom we share it with, and inform you about the controls we give you to access,
change or deactivate your account information.

Our Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding personal information (“Personal Data”) that we
collect from users through our websites, the Wonolo mobile application, the Wonolo platform, and 
related media or services.

The important thing we want you to know is that we use information collected only for the purpose of
providing and improving the Service to you or others who use Wonolo’s services. This Privacy Policy is
incorporated into, and considered a part of, the Terms of Service or Customer Agreement that you
agree to, when you use our Service. If you have questions about anything you read here, just contact
us (https://help.wonolo.com/s/contactsupport).

2. Information We Collect

We collect Personal Data in the following ways: (1) information you provide, (2) information we get 
when you use our Service, and (3) information we collect from users and third parties.

Information You Provide

We collect information from you when you interact with our Service. The specific Personal Data we
collect is dependent on how you use our Service.

If you use our Service as a Wonoloer, we collect information needed to onboard you, run background
check (if required by a job you accept), notify you of potentially suitable jobs, provide you with
information and updates relating to jobs, confirm your arrival and departure from accepted jobs, and
to coordinate payment to you for work performed.

If you use our Service as a Customer, we collect information needed to open a customer account,
promote the short-term assignments you have for Wonoloers, provide you with updates relating to
those assignments, and charge you for the work performed by the Wonoloer.

Information We Get When You Use Our Service

When you use our Service, we collect information about how you’ve used it. We collect information
when you visit our website, use our mobile applications, use our platform, interact with a Customer’s
website, or communicate with us through our customer support teams. We’ll collect whatever 
information you volunteer or that we need to resolve your question.
