How Rosterd works

Rosterd for organizations

Want to sign up?

Get started with 4 easy steps

Sign Up

Register your organization by filling in form on our website. It will take just a few moments.

Recieve access

After you have signed up, our dedicated team will help setting your organization up and give access to your Rosterd platform.

Send invite

Send email invite to all your staff members to download Rosterd app either on iphone or android.

Post your job

You are all set! Post your first job and let your staff imediately know about it. Enjoy simplicity!
