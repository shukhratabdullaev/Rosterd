We are Rosterd

Automate rosters and find shift workers when needed.

Rosterd is a mobile app and platform with Powerful AI to match the right staff for the job. It helps companies automate their rosters and shift workers, Rosterd also helps its employees find jobs at the right company without having to go through any intermediary or HR professional. It’s easy to use on any device, and comes with everything the company and their staff need to manage shifts.

Rosterd's tech-driven approach to staffing provides an efficient way for both job seekers and employers to connect directly.

Rosterd will make it easier than ever before for companies to save time on managing rosters. They can now focus on what matters most – running the business. With Rosterd, you can create a custom schedule for your employees and send a push notification to them on their phone about their schedule!

Rosterd was founded in 2021 by tech entrepreneurs who saw first-hand the pain point of shift working, leading them to develop a service that makes it easy for people needing to switch jobs or work shifts at short notice, while enabling businesses owners to quickly hire reliable talent on demand.
